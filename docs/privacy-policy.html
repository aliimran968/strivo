Strivo

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 19, 2026

1. Overview

Strivo ("the App") is a mobile focus and productivity application developed by Ali Imran ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using Strivo, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create a Strivo account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name (provided by you)
  • Encrypted password (managed by Supabase Auth — we never see your raw password)

Focus Session Data

When you use the focus timer, we store:

  • Session duration (in seconds)
  • Session tag (e.g. Coding, Design, Writing)
  • Date and time the session was completed
  • XP and coin balances earned over time

Device & Usage Data

We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, crash logs, or analytics data beyond what is described above. We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Notification Preferences

If you enable daily reminders, we store your preferred reminder time locally on your device only. We do not transmit this to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Provide and operate the App (syncing your sessions across devices)
  • Display your focus history, XP progress, and streak data
  • Send optional daily reminder notifications (only if you enable them)
  • Restore your account and data if you log in on a new device

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not run advertisements.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your account and session data is stored using Supabase, a cloud database platform hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS). Supabase implements row-level security, meaning your data is only accessible by your own authenticated account.

Session data is also cached locally on your device using AsyncStorage as a fallback for offline use.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

5. Third-Party Services

Strivo uses the following third-party service:

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — authentication and database storage. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

We do not integrate with Facebook, Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or any advertising networks.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address listed in Section 10.

You can also clear all locally stored data from within the App using the "Reset App Data" option on the onboarding screen. Note that this only removes local data — to delete your account from our servers entirely, please contact us directly.

7. Children's Privacy

Strivo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws (such as GDPR or CCPA) including:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the Effective Date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
